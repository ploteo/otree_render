Given your current grade and monetary prize, according to the Italian university system, you have two options.

You can choose to either accept your grade along with the corresponding prize you have earned, or reject the grade.

Rejecting the grade will enter you into a lottery designed to simulate a scenario where you decide to reject the result of an exam and retake it.

The retake exam can lead to three different outcomes that have all the same probability.
